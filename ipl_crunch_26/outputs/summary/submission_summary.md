# IPL CRUNCH '26 Submission Summary

Dataset used: latest five seasons available in the JSON folder, 2022-2026.
Matches analysed: 356 completed matches.

## Answer 1: Do toss winners win more matches?

Toss winners won 50.6% of completed matches; toss losers won 49.4%.

## Answer 2: Which phase is most linked to winning?

The biggest winner-loser scoring gap was in the middle overs: winning teams averaged 8.9 more runs than losing teams in that phase.
